How to Play Retro Bowl

Retro Bowl is a fun and exciting game where you get to be both the coach and the quarterback! In this colorful, classic football world, your goal is to score more points than your opponents by making smart plays, moving the ball downfield, and reaching the end zone. Here's how you can become a champion:

1. Pick Your Play
Select a run, pass, or kick play from the sidelines. If you're on a PC, use the arrow keys; if you're on a mobile device, tap your choice.

2. Snap and Go
When the ball is snapped, hold the down arrow key (or tap and hold) to speed up your runner. You can also press the spacebar (or tap) to throw a pass to your teammates.

3. Avoid Tackles
Help your player dodge defenders by quickly changing directions. Tap or press the arrow keys to avoid being tackled and stay within the playing field.

4. Score and Extra Points
Get into the end zone to score a touchdown, and then kick the ball through the uprights to earn extra points.

5. Build Your Dynasty
After each game, manage your team by signing new rookies, training players, and improving their skills. Keep track of statistics and adjust strategies to rise the leaderboard and win the Retro Bowl trophy!

 

Controls

Use the left and right arrow keys to move around the field. Press the up arrow to jump over defenders or catch high passes. To slide safely under rushing opponents, press the down arrow.

When you're on offense, hold the spacebar to make a speedy dash that can break through the line. Want to pass the ball down the field? Press Z to send a perfect pass to your teammate.
