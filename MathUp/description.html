How to Play Mathup

Are you ready for an exciting number adventure? In Mathup, you'll see a big target number at the top of your screen and a mix of number and operator tiles below. Your task is to choose the tiles that exactly add up to the target number. For example, if the target is 10, you can tap "2," "+," and "8" to make 10! When you get it right, those tiles disappear, and new ones fall into place. Keep clearing all the tiles before the countdown clock reaches zero to win the level and unlock a more challenging one.

As you play, you might find helpful tools that let you shuffle the board, freeze time, or blast away tricky tiles—so use them wisely! Mathup turns simple math problems into an exciting brain challenge. Grab your mouse or tap your screen, solve as many equations as you can, and watch your score grow!
