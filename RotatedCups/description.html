How to Play Rotated Cups

Are you ready to improve your memory with a fun and fast-paced cup shuffle game? Here's how to play:

1. Start by arranging three cups in a row. Hide a small ball under one of the cups.

2. Watch carefully as the cups slide around, swap places, and spin on the table.

3. When the shuffling stops, tap the cup where you believe the ball is hidden.

If you guess correctly, you earn a point, and the next round becomes even faster. This will test your focus and quick reaction! Did you pick the wrong cup? Don't worry. Jump back in and try again to beat your high score.

This exciting brain teaser is perfect for kids, helping to boost observation skills and concentration playfully. Are you up for the challenge? Keep your eyes on the prize, and see how many points you can collect before time runs out!
