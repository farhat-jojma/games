How to Play Cup Stacking

Become a master at the cup stacking game with some simple keyboard moves! Use the ← and → arrow keys to slide your cup into the perfect spot beneath the tower. Once it’s perfectly aligned, press the spacebar to drop it neatly on top. Be careful to keep your timing just right—if you stack too quickly or too slowly, the tower might start to wobble. If your tower falls, press R to restart or P to pause and take a break. No downloads are needed—open your browser and enjoy this free cup stacking game designed for kids!
