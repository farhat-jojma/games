How to Play Math Tiles: A Super-Fun Guide for Kids

Get ready to dash through colorful number blocks in Math Tiles, a thrilling math game perfect for young learners! Here's how to excel at each round:

Spot the Equation

At the top, you will see a simple math problem, like 8 + 4 or 10 − 2. Your mission is to find and pick the tile with the correct answer.

Tap to Score

Select the correct number block and watch it burst! Each popped tile adds to your points. Be careful! If you tap the wrong block, you will lose a few seconds.

Watch the Stack

Tiles slowly stack up from below. If they touch the top line, it’s game over. Keep clearing the tiles quickly to stay in the game.

Grab Power-Ups

Use bomb tiles to clear nearby blocks instantly and collect clock tokens to gain extra seconds on the timer.

Aim for Combos

Solve several problems in a row without mistakes to earn streak bonuses. The longer your combo, the bigger your score boost!

Your goal is simple: solve as many math problems as you can before time runs out, rack up combos, and climb to the top of the leaderboard. Have fun learning and racing against the clock in Math Tiles!
