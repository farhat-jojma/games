How to Play Color Water Sort

Welcome to Color Water Sort, the exciting puzzle game where you mix liquid rainbows! Here’s how to be a master at it:

1. Tap on a tube to lift its top layer of colored water.

2. Slide that stream into another tube, but only if the colors match or if the tube is empty.

3. Keep pouring, one splash at a time, until each tube is filled with just one solid color from top to bottom.

Tip for Tiny Tacticians: Always look for empty tubes to make room, and plan your moves a step or two ahead to avoid getting stuck. With a little thinking ahead, you'll be turning swirling colors into neat towers in no time. Enjoy creating your very own liquid rainbow!
