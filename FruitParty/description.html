Get ready for a colorful match-3 adventure! In Fruit Party, you'll explore a grid filled with juicy apples, bananas, grapes, and watermelons. Your mission is simple: swap two fruits to line up three or more identical ones in a row. When they match, they burst into exciting sparks, and you earn points!

Keep an eye on your number of moves and score meter. Each level has a target score you must reach before you run out of swaps. Along the way, special power-ups appear: match four fruits to create a Fruit Bomb that clears nearby pieces, or align five to create a Rainbow Slice that wipes out an entire row or column.

As you achieve your goals, you'll collect stars and unlock new, more exciting boards. Watch the fruits rain down, plan your swaps wisely, and celebrate every sweet victory.
