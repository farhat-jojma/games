Controls

Get ready to guide your brave little cup through an exciting platform adventure!

• Tap the left (←) and right (→) arrow keys to roll your cup across each level.

• Press and hold the spacebar to jump over gaps. Hold it longer for even higher jumps!

• Slide under low fences by pressing the down (↓) arrow or the “S” key.
