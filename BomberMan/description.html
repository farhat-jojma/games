How to Play BomberMan

Jump into the colorful world of BomberMan and get ready for nonstop fun! In this maze-style game, your job is to place bombs, clear obstacles, and outsmart goofy monsters—all before the timer runs out. Here's how to become a BomberMan pro:

1. Move Around: Use the arrow keys on your keyboard or swipe on a touchscreen to navigate through the grid-like levels.

2. Drop a Bomb: Hit the spacebar (or tap the bomb button) to plant a bomb behind you. Then dash away quickly so you won't be caught in the blast!

3. Smash Crates: Explosions break open wooden crates. Inside, you can find awesome power-ups—extra bombs, bigger explosions, or speedy boots that help you zoom past enemies.

4. Defeat or Escape: In each stage, either clear every silly monster or locate the glowing exit door. Grab as many bonuses as you can to boost your score.

5. Watch the Clock: Every round has a time limit, so think quickly and move even faster!

With smart planning and quick feet, you'll rack up high scores and become the ultimate BomberMan champion in no time!
