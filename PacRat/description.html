Controls

Are you ready to guide Pac Rat through an exciting online maze adventure? Here's how you can play right in your browser—no downloads needed!

• Use the ← and → arrows to move left and right as you chase every piece of cheese.

• Press the ↑ arrow key or the Spacebar to jump high—perfect for leaping over deep pits and sneaky cats.

• Stand next to a hidden wall and press ↓ to slip through secret passages and discover bonus cheese.

With these simple Pac Rat controls, even young players can jump right into the fun!
