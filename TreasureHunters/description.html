How to Play Treasure Hunters

Are you ready for an exciting adventure? In "Treasure Hunters," you become a brave explorer on a mission to collect as much treasure as possible before time runs out. Here's a simple step-by-step guide to get you started:

1. Move Your Explorer

Desktop: Use the ← ↑ → ↓ arrow keys.
Mobile: Tap the on-screen arrows.
Guide your hero through dense jungles, dark caves, and mysterious pirate ships. Be careful—there are hidden traps and rolling rocks ready to send you back!

2. Collect Shiny Treasures

As you explore, pick up glittering gems, shiny coins, and special keys. Each treasure you find increases your score and brings you closer to victory. Remember, the more you collect, the higher you'll rank on the leaderboard!

3. Unlock Treasure Chests

When you find a treasure chest, press the jump button (or tap the chest) to open it. Inside, you might discover bonus coins, rare gems, or secret power-ups to help you speed through the level even faster.

4. Reach the Finish Portal

Your ultimate goal is to reach the glowing portal at the end of each stage. Dash, dodge, and dive past obstacles to get there before the timer runs out. The faster you finish, the larger your final treasure haul will be!
