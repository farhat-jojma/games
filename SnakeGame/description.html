Tips and Tricks for the Snake Game

Do you want to master the Snake Game like a real expert? Here's how! First, always plan your moves. Look ahead and decide where your snake will turn before it moves. This will help avoid crashing into your tail. Next, aim for those special fruits—the shiny apples and stars. They're not only beautiful; they make your snake grow and earn you extra points!

If you encounter a narrow tunnel, it's better to slide along the edge rather than rushing through the middle. This gives you more space to turn around corners. As your snake becomes longer, take slow and careful steps. Moving slowly can help you steer clear of tricky spots.
